Marcus Aurelius

Why would a Roman Emperor ruling over millions spend his nights writing private notes to himself about humility,self control and death?. These werent speeches or public letters they werent meant for anyone else, just for him alone with his thoughts. 2000 years later his words still echo on motivation quotes, in thearpy sessions and on bookshelves. The person was no other than Marcus Aurelius and the notes he wrote to himself were called as Meditations.
